Kindergarten (2021) — The Beginning ⭐⭐
As soon as Rish began speaking clearly, his storytelling journey naturally began. He loved expressing his ideas and feelings, weaving beautiful little stories around everything he explored and narrating them with excitement.
The Bear and The Mouse
A gentle story of friendship between a bear and a mouse, celebrating kindness and helping others.
This book is his very first handmade creation — a heartfelt story he narrated in his own words and illustrated himself, while the story was lovingly written down as Rish was still learning to write.
This little book marked the beginning of his storytelling journey in written form, filled with imagination, creativity, and joy.
Created and illustrated by Rish.
The Fairyland
A touching story of friendship between a young girl and her beloved pet, reflecting selfless love, compassion, and courage.
As Rish began learning to write, he took his next big step with The Fairyland. This was his first published book where he both wrote and illustrated the story himself — a proud milestone in his early author journey.
This story follows a young child and a magical world of imagination, adventure, and wonder, highlighting the creativity and confidence that began shaping his storytelling voice.
Written and illustrated by Rish by hand.
Available internationally, including in India, in Kindle edition.
